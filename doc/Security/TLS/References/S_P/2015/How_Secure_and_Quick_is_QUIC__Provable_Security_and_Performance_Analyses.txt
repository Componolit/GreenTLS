====== How Secure and Quick is QUIC? Provable Security and Performance Analyses ======

- https://eprint.iacr.org/2015/582.pdf

QUIC does not provide forward secrecy.
QUIC is prone to attacks preventing 0-RTT and DoS attacks.
There is a trade off between latency and security.
