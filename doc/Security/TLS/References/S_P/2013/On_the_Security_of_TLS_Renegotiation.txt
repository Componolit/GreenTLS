====== On the Security of TLS Renegotiation ======

- https://eprint.iacr.org/2012/630.pdf

The renegotiation mechanism of TLS version 1.2 and prior allow MITM attacks.
Aside disabling renegotiation, two countermeasures were standardized: RIE and SCSV.
It was formally shown that the proposed fixes offer good protection against renegotiation attacks.
